About us

Shanghai EFE Eyewear Corp Ltd. founded in 2008, the headquarter located in the fashion capital, Shanghai.

EFE operation center is over 6000 square meters, which is one of the biggest center in the optical industry.

EFE is the first eyewear enterprise listed on Shanghai Stock Exchange since 2015 .

It is a fashion brand specializing in good quality eyewear products with very attractive prices.

Rapid updating new styles to fulfill the needs of different markets.

We have a strong and experienced after sales services team, with average 6 years experiences in the industry, to ensure the best

service being provided to our customers.

Our strength

We are a direct supplier and producer of wholesales optical, sunglasses and reading glasses, We also carry quality discount

eyewear for retailers who want to sell optical, sunglasses and reading glasses for profit.

With wide range of collections in different materials, like acetate, TR90, titanium, monel, stainless steel, aluminum. Of course also

the good combination with different materials, like acetate with monel temples, TR90 mixed with titanium or monel, stainless steel

with TR90 temples, etc.

You can easily select the need frames for your customers from EFE collections, from regular optical frames in bulk to wholesale

designer frames. We also carry lots of sport sunglasses for the active lifestyles, as well as the high fashion types who want to

have the stylish look.

Profit Marking Collections

As former retailer, we understand the importance of having a product line that retailers can trust. Consumers need to feel confident that

they are purchasing eyewear is of good quality for a very good price. Not only will you find top quality styles in our inventory, but you will

also see the hottest trends in what people are wearing.

All of this, and you will find our pricing to be very competitive, especially with the volume discount pricing. Take a look at our vast

selection of styles and see why more and more retailers are turning to our product line to carry in their stores.
